A Pursuit Lead must reach out to the Pursuit QVA Reviewer as soon as they are assigned.

And, the QVA Reviewer should ask the Lead about the availability of the solution …

…and fix time for the review.

The QVA Reviewer must request for a copy of the current pursuit deliverables that include QSRM, solution approach or methodology, staffing plan, Pricing Model, and proposal or Request for proposal (or RFP), if required.

The Pursuit Lead must provide QVA Reviewer with all the documents and ensure time in hand to incorporate review recommendations, prior to proposal submission.

The Pursuit Lead also provides the pursuit pinnacle ID or PID to charge their time and Travel and Living or T&L, if applicable.
