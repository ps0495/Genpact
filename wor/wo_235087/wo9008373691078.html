Throughout the lifecycle of a deal, you may conduct QVA reviews at different phases.

When a deal is flagged for a QVA review, you must conduct the QVA review prior to the proposal submission and while the deal is in the Defined phase.

The QVA Reviewer reviews the defined solution with staffing and pricing, and provides recommendations.

If the QVA review recommends a material change in scope to the solution or in pricing, then you may request a follow-up QVA review.

This secondary review is optional. The Pursuit Lead, LCP, or Vertical Leader decides whether to conduct the secondary review.

Lastly, the QVA Reviewer conducts a review prior to signing the deal and during the Confirmed phase. The QVA review ensures that the commercial arrangement aligns with the proposed solution.
