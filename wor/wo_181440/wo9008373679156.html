Wave 2, Release 1 introduces Quality and Value Assurance (or QVA) review to improve how we pursue and deliver Transformation Services.

QVA is an independent review to validate whether we are delivering value against client expectations.

During a pursuit, the QVA program provides assurance that the proposed solution meets client’s business value objectives, and aligns with our growth and strategy goals.

During project execution, QVA provides assurance that the project performs in accordance with the commercial arrangement and client expectations.

Therefore, with the QVA program in place, we will be aligned with our clients during both the sales and delivery processes.

In addition, experienced and certified QVA TS Partners, who act as advisors to the client project team and the client, as applicable, conduct the QVA reviews.

There are two key reasons why we want to leverage the QVA program. One, to deliver client value, and two, to leverage internal learnings.
