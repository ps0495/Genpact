The QVA review is initiated with the QVA Reviewer, Pursuit Lead, Opportunity Lead (or Seller), and Pursuit team. LCP’s participation is optional.

The initial review should take a minimum of 90 minutes to 120 minutes, or more, depending on the complexity of the opportunity.

So, how should you structure the same?

You should discuss the four major topics.

  • Client need and our value proposition
  • Solution approach or methodology and deliverables,
  • Staffing model, and
  • Pricing model.

These topics can be discussed in any order, but should be discussed at length.

The QVA Reviewer should also reference the QSRM scoring, contributing to an amber or red rating.
