The Pursuit Lead now needs to review any recommendations or actions outlined in the QVA Review Memo and take action.

If the recommendation is “option 2: Proceed with Conditions,” the Pursuit Lead must ensure the appropriate follow up that may require subsequent reviews. The Pursuit Lead needs to update solution approach, staffing model, and/or commercial model, based on review actions.

The Pursuit Lead discusses with the Vertical Lead and requests for approval on the completed actions, for moving the opportunity to On-Bid.

If the recommendation is “option 3: Do Not Proceed,” then the Pursuit lead has several actions to be taken.

The Pursuit Lead must discuss the recommendations with the Vertical Lead and determine what senior leader approval/escalation is required if the intent is to proceed with the proposal submission. That means, even if the recommendation is “Not to Proceed”, the system will not allow the Seller, the LCP, or the Pursuit Lead to submit the opportunity to On-Bid. This is to ensure that the senior leadership has an opportunity to discuss whether the opportunity should proceed in its current form or it should be re-worked from a solution, staffing, or pricing standpoint. If the leadership decides to proceed with the opportunity in its current form, then the Vertical Lead will need to approve it on SFDC for the deal to move to On-Bid. Alternatively, the Vertical Lead can recommend that the solution be re-worked and re-submitted for the QVA review.
